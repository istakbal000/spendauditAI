# Go-to-Market Strategy — AI Spend Audit

## Target Audience

**Primary:** Startup CTOs and Engineering Leads (5–100 person companies)
**Secondary:** Founders managing their own tools budget
**Tertiary:** Finance / Ops at Series A–B companies auditing tooling costs

## Value Proposition

"Stop overpaying for AI tools. Audit your stack in 60 seconds."

Core promise: Find wasted AI spend with zero friction (no login, instant results, free).

## Distribution Channels

### Organic / Viral
- Product Hunt launch (target #1 Product of the Day)
- Hacker News "Show HN" post
- Reddit: r/startups, r/SaaS, r/ExperiencedDevs
- Twitter/X: Dev and founder communities
- LinkedIn: SaaS and startup founders

### SEO
- Target: "ai tools cost", "cursor vs copilot price", "chatgpt team plan worth it"
- Long-tail: "[tool] cheaper alternative", "reduce ai subscription costs"
- Build comparison content: "Cursor vs Copilot: which is cheaper for your team size"

### Partnerships
- Startup accelerators (YC, Techstars) — offer as free tool for portfolio
- CFO/finance communities
- Engineering newsletters (TLDR, Pointer, Bytes)

## Conversion Funnel

```
Traffic → Landing Page → Run Audit → See Results → Email Capture → Consultation CTA
```

Key metric: Landing → Audit conversion rate (target >40%)
Key metric: Audit → Email capture rate (target >25%)
Key metric: Email → Consultation booked rate (target >5% for high-savings users)
