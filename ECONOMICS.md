# Economics — AI Spend Audit

## Unit Economics Model

### Free Tool (Lead Gen Model)

**Cost to serve:**
- Vercel hosting: ~$0 (Hobby plan for low traffic, Pro at $20/mo for scale)
- Supabase: Free tier (500MB, 2GB transfer) → $25/mo at scale
- Gemini API: ~$0.075/1M tokens (Flash) → effectively free at low volume
- Resend: Free tier (3,000 emails/mo) → $20/mo for 50,000 emails
- Domain + CDN: ~$15/mo

**Total infrastructure cost:** ~$0 (free tier) → ~$80/mo at meaningful scale

### Revenue Model

**Consultation upsell:** High-savings users (>$500/mo savings found) see a "Book Free Consultation" CTA.

Assumed conversion rates:
- 1,000 monthly visitors
- 35% run an audit → 350 audits/mo
- 40% of audits find >$500/mo savings → 140 high-savings users
- 8% book a consultation → ~11 consultations/mo
- 30% close into paid engagements → ~3 clients/mo

**Average deal value:** $2,000 (one-time AI stack optimization engagement)
**Monthly revenue potential:** 3 × $2,000 = $6,000/mo at 1K visitors

### Scaling

| Monthly Visitors | Audits | Consultations | Revenue |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1,000 | 350 | 11 | ~$6K |
| 10,000 | 3,500 | 110 | ~$60K |
| 50,000 | 17,500 | 550 | ~$330K |

### Alternative Revenue Models

1. **SaaS subscription:** $29/mo for saved history, team sharing, alerts
2. **API/white-label:** License the audit engine to HR/finance tools
3. **Affiliate:** Referral commissions when users switch to recommended tools
4. **Sponsored slots:** AI tools pay to be "recommended" (disclosed)
