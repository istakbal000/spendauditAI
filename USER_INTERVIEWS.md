# User Interviews — AI Spend Audit

## Interview Guide

Use these questions when speaking to potential users (startup CTOs, founders, engineers).

### Screener
- How many people are on your team?
- Do you use any AI tools? Which ones?
- Who manages the AI tools budget?

### Discovery Questions

1. "Walk me through how your team decides which AI tools to pay for."
2. "Have you ever cancelled an AI subscription because it wasn't worth it? What happened?"
3. "How do you currently track what you spend on AI tools?"
4. "Have you ever discovered you were paying for overlapping tools? How did that happen?"
5. "What would need to be true for you to trust a free tool's savings recommendation?"

### Problem Validation

6. "On a scale of 1–10, how confident are you that your current AI spend is optimized?"
7. "If you found out you were wasting $500/month on AI subscriptions, what would you do?"
8. "Who would you share an audit result with internally?"

### Solution Validation

9. "What would make you immediately trust or distrust savings recommendations from a tool like this?"
10. "Would you share a tool like this with your network? Under what conditions?"
